<constant> obp:bibliography-added :string

Event obp:bibliography-added. Raised by the openbibl/filter and openbibl/sort controllers to signal a change in the visibility or ordering of bibliography entries; triggers recalculation and/or re-initialization in the highlight, TOC, and tooltip modules. Re-initialization would be required due to a change in the Document requiring re-setting DOM event handlers.
